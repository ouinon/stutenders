- WELCOME TO STU TENDERS - BOAT HIRE -

Two Wallys up-to speed

- 35 Years of Experience -

In 2010 and after over 35 years of yachting experience Captain Stewart Sanderson and his team founded Stu Tenders out of a desire to provide a service that couldn’t be found elsewhere on the stunning coasts of the French Riviera. Since then the company has expanded to service a wide area ranging from St Tropez in the West to well beyond the Italian border in the East and as far South as Corsica.

Intimate local knowledge of the coasts, the team can provide local knowledge on exclusive beaches, local anchorages as well as Beach Clubs and Bars.

- The Perfect Tender -

With boats ranging from 45 feet to 32 feet.The Wally Tender is the choice of many a Superyacht, should a yachts own tender be otherwise occupied, discerning guests will regularly stipulate “Wally Tender”. The striking lines of the Van Dutch make it the last word in stylish boat design, proving a head-turner at the St Tropez Beach Clubs. The Monte Carlo 32 features a small cabin and twin 220 hp gasoline engines.

- EXCLUSIVE SERVICES -

From private day and weekly crewed charter whatever the duration and whatever your demands, we ensure the comfortable transport of you and your guests in style. A private tender provides ultimate freedom to discerning clients and allows access to areas otherwise inaccessible by boat.

The tenders also serve to provide a relaxing platform on the water for swimming snorkelling or simply enjoying the thrill of the waves on a performance boat. The every tender in our fleet was selected to give the client extreme ease of embarking and disembarking, from dock or yacht. They truly provide the perfect platform for all coastal leisure including cabins for privacy and refrigeration for those cool sundowners.

- SERVICES FOR SUPERYACHTS -

In addition to day rental boats the company services Superyachts with everything that even the most demanding of vessels could need. From ship to port transfers to airport transfers, the safe transportation of guests and crew is always assured.

With boats of up to 45 feet individual vessels can already comfortably accommodate up to 8 guests, for larger parties a fleet can be arranged. Stewart and his team are well known in the industry and always receive a warm welcome, whatever the port guests can feel at ease around a friendly entourage.
